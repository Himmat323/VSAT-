(IPP)Independent Power Producers

A Independent Power Producer (IPP) in the context of VSAT Renewable refers to a private entity that
develops, owns, and operates renewable energy projects—such as solar parks and wind farms—and sells electricity to utilities, industries, or direct consumers.

Role of IPP in VSAT Renewables
1. Renewable Energy generation – Develops and manages large-scale solar, wind, and hybrid power plan
2. Grid & Off-Grid Solutions – Supplies electricity to national grids or directly to businesses and remote communities.
3. Power Purchase Agreements (PPA) – Engages in long-term contracts with utilities or corporate buyers for energy supply.
4. Sustainability & Carbon Reduction – Supports the transition t clean energy , reducing dependence on fossil fuels.
5. Investment & innovation – Attracts funding for new technologies, battery storage, and efficient power distribution.

ENERGY STORAGE

Round-the-clock (RTC) power, as the name suggests, literally means power supply that is available 24×7, throughout the year. Renewable energy supply round-the-clock (RTC) with the help of battery storage overcomes the intermittency associated with solar, which generate energy only when there is enough sunshine. Energy Storage solution has built-in Li-Ion battery with Power Conditioning Unit (PCU).
A plug-and-play energy storage platform
AH-Stack is a flexible,modular, plug-and-play battery energy storage platform for a wide variety of application ranging from 25kw-2 hour systems to 25MW - 4 hours systems.
